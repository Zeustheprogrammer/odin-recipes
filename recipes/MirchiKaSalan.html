Mirchi Ka Salan

Description

Mirchi Ka Salan is a spicy and tangy curry made with long green chilies, peanuts, sesame seeds, and coconut. It's a delicious accompaniment to biryani, rice, or roti.

Ingredients

  • Long green chilies
  • Peanuts
  • Sesame seeds
  • Coconut
  • Tamarind
  • Onion
  • Mustard seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • Red chili powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Roast peanuts, sesame seeds, and coconut separately; grind into a paste with tamarind.
  2. Fry green chilies until blistered.
  3. In a pan, heat oil, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, and sliced onions; saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Add the ground paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates.
  5. Add the fried green chilies to the mixture and simmer until flavors blend.
  6. Serve hot with biryani or rice.
