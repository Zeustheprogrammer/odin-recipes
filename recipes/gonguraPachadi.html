Gongura Pachadi

Description

Gongura Pachadi is a tangy and spicy chutney made with sorrel leaves, red and green chilies, garlic, and tamarind. It's a traditional Telangana condiment that adds a burst of flavors to your meal.

Ingredients

  • Gongura (sorrel leaves)
  • Red and green chilies
  • Garlic cloves
  • Tamarind
  • Urad dal (black gram)
  • Mustard seeds
  • Cumin seeds
  • Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Clean and chop gongura leaves.
  2. Sauté gongura leaves, red and green chilies, and garlic in oil.
  3. Grind the sautéed mixture with tamarind to make a coarse paste.
  4. Temper mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and urad dal in a separate pan.
  5. Add the tempering to the chutney and mix well.
  6. Serve with hot rice or as a side dish.
