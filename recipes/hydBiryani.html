Hyderabadi Biryani

Description

A fragrant and flavorful rice dish layered with marinated meat, aromatic spices, and basmati rice, cooked to perfection. Hyderabadi Biryani is a classic and beloved dish with roots in the royal kitchens of Hyderabad.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken or mutton pieces
  • Onions
  • Yogurt
  • Ghee (clarified butter)
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Biryani masala
  • Mint and coriander leaves
  • Saffron strands (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Marinate the meat in yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and biryani masala.
  2. Fry onions until golden brown.
  3. Layer marinated meat, partially cooked basmati rice, fried onions, and mint-coriander leaves in a pot.
  4. Cook on low heat until the rice and meat are fully cooked.
  5. Garnish with saffron-infused milk. Serve hot with raita.
